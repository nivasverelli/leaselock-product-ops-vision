# A Vision for Product Operations at LeaseLock

A single-page interactive website built to share my perspective 
on the Product Operations Analyst role at LeaseLock — what I'd 
focus on, how I'd approach it, and where I see it going in an 
AI-first world.

## Why I Built This

During my interview prep for LeaseLock, I went deep on the 
problem space — certificate management, PMS integrations, 
Yardi/RealPage data flows, exception patterns, Databricks 
infrastructure. Instead of letting that research sit in a 
notes file, I turned it into something shareable.

This isn't a formal proposal. It's a point of view.

## What's Inside

- **Phase One** — Why I'd spend the first six months learning 
  before building anything
- **The Decision Log** — Capturing institutional knowledge 
  from day one
- **From Manual to Intelligent** — The AI classifier vision 
  built on LeaseLock's existing Databricks stack
- **The Scale Argument** — Why manual ops and portfolio growth 
  eventually pull against each other
- **Personal Perspective** — What it means to grow up during 
  the AI shift and why domain knowledge is still the 
  scarce resource
